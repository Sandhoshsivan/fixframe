The Studio

We are a crew,
not a studio.

FixFrame was born in Tamil Nadu with a simple belief — that India's richest stories deserve a crew that understands them from the inside. We are photographers, cinematographers, editors, drone pilots, and artists who grew up in these cultures.

From the grand temples of Madurai to the palaces of Rajasthan, from intimate Chennai weddings to Himalayan brand campaigns — our crew travels across India. We handle everything: photography, cinematic films, pre-wedding shoots, event coverage, and even bespoke digital wedding cards and invitation suites.
